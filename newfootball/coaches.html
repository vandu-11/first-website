Meet Our Football Coaches

At our football academy, we have a team of experienced and skilled coaches who are dedicated to helping young footballers improve their skills and reach their full potential. Get to know our coaches and their areas of expertise:

John Smith - Head Coach

John has been coaching football for over 20 years and holds a UEFA Pro license. He has worked with professional football teams both in Europe and North America, and has also coached youth teams at various levels. John specializes in tactical analysis and team strategy, and is passionate about helping young players develop their decision-making skills on the pitch..


Maria Gonzalez - Assistant Coach

Maria is a former professional football player who has represented her country at the international level. She holds a UEFA A license and has been coaching for 10 years. Maria is an expert in player development and focuses on helping players improve their technical skills, such as dribbling, passing, and shooting..


David Brown - Goalkeeper Coach

David is a former professional goalkeeper who has played for several top-tier teams. He holds a UEFA B license and has been coaching goalkeepers for 15 years. David specializes in developing goalkeeper-specific skills, such as positioning, handling, and distribution..


Sarah Lee - Fitness Coach

Sarah is a certified strength and conditioning coach who specializes in sports performance training. She has worked with athletes from various sports, including football, and has helped them improve their speed, agility, and endurance. Sarah works closely with our football coaches to develop customized training programs that help our players stay fit and healthy throughout the season.


Mark Johnson - Mental Skills Coach

Mark is a licensed sports psychologist who specializes in mental skills training for athletes. He has worked with professional footballers from around the world and has helped them develop strategies to improve their focus, confidence, and resilience. Mark works with our players to help them overcome mental obstacles and perform at their best on the pitch..
